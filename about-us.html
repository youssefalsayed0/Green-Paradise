About Us

Are you looking for a place that combines sports and entertainment? At Green Paradise, we offer everything you need, from football fields, tennis courts, padel courts, equestrian areas, archery, and more, to enjoy a unique experience.

  • Kids' Play Area: Learn and Play We provide a safe and fun environment for children where they can play and explore freely.
  • Variety of Restaurants: Enjoy dining in a selection of restaurants offering dishes to suit all tastes, from traditional meals to global cuisines.
  • Wedding and Event Halls: Celebrate your special occasions in one of our unique halls, offering personalized services to make your day unforgettable.
  • Relaxation Area: Enjoy by the fountain with delicious meals and drinks at the resort’s various restaurants and cafes.
  • Cultural and Artistic Events: Experience unique performances, including musical and theatrical shows, at our theater, celebrating culture and the arts.

We don't just offer a park; we create an unforgettable experience for every visitor. Whether you're looking for a fun day with family or a special time with friends, Green Paradise is the perfect choice.

Join us at Green Paradise and enjoy the best moments where fun, sports, and health come together in one place! Don’t miss the chance to visit this fantastic destination and let us be part of your beautiful memories.
